Welcome to the Poison the Mind Memorial

This archive preserves the creative works and community history of poisonthemind.com, a vibrant creative community that thrived from 2004 to 2010. Here you'll find original fiction, artwork, and the stories of the talented individuals who made this community special.

Founded by Matthew "Bitterman" Hall, Poison the Mind was a place where creativity flourished—from post-apocalyptic fiction to dark fantasy, from art to comics. This memorial ensures that their work lives on.
