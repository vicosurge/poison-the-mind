Sanctusorium: Its HDR photography. Basically, its five different exposures of the same tree combined. Two underexposed, two over, and one just right. Mixed them together with photomatix and you got a shot that has the best highlights and the best shadows all in one =) The reason it is so fuzzy is because of the the fact I had to take five different shots... They are kinda out of place and the top of the neck is almost impossible to reach but the knobs are actually still usable because the strings go around the back of the neck... which actually makes it more impossible to play the first frets but fuck it. Enjoy!
Admiral_Dread: Now, everyone... Bow before your new god, Basonimus!
AmmoJack: I dig the body art, but from a musician's standpoint the design is pretty impractical. Main points are the fretboard, which goes all the way to the bridge, which not only wouldn't be accessable for playing, but also blocks any space for pickups and the tuning pegs working the way they do, which would cause for a mega heavy head (for gnarly neck dive) and also likely heavy tuning instability with a lot of string breaks from the heavy pressure at the nut....looks cool as hell though, I'd just like to see the idea applied to a second more realistic body/neck design!
