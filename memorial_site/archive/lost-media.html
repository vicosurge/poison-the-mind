Do You Have These Files?

If you were a member of the Poison the Mind community and downloaded any of these audio files, we would be grateful if you could share them with us. These creative works deserve to be preserved and made available once again.

How to help:

  • Check your old computers, backup drives, or CD/DVD archives from 2007-2010
  • Search for MP3 files with the filenames listed above
  • Look for folders named "poisonthemind" or "PTM" in your downloads
  • Contact the archive maintainer if you find anything

Even if you only have one or two files, every piece helps preserve this community's creative legacy.
